## Overview

Pastel is a mod about color magic, exploration, and discovery. You... are not reading about the mod in the repo.
If you want the synopsis just go to the curseforge page.

## Addon Making

Feel free to make content relating to the mod. Addons aren't just welcome but encouraged, we will happily provide API 
support and clarify any matters regarding themes and progression if needed. The more things Pastel the better!

The only thing that we ask is that you do not call whatever you are making Stargazer or Eventide for... reasons.

# Pastel's Discord
Want to discuss about the mod, its development, or about making content for it? Head to the Terrarium discord - the 
Pastel channels there are the hub of the mod, and where you will find the devs.

https://discord.gg/terrarium

## Mod Integration
- AE2: Lots of additional Recipes; Crystal Apothecary compat
- Botania: New custom items, Black Hole Talisman support, additional recipes & Potion Workshop reagents
- Create: Additional recipes
